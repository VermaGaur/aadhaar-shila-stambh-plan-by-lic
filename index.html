About LIC Aadhar Shila and Aadhar Stambh Plans

The LIC Aadhar Shila and Aadhar Stambh Plans are special insurance policies that are linked to the Aadhaar card. These plans are designed to provide financial protection to individuals and their families in case of unfortunate events such as death or disability.

Benefits of LIC Aadhar Shila and Aadhar Stambh Plans

  • Death benefit
  • Accidental death and disability benefit
  • Maturity benefit
  • Tax benefits
  • Loan facility
  • Surrender value
  • Flexible premium payment options
  • High sum assured rebate
  • Bonus
  • Guaranteed additions
  • Terminal bonus

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age of entry: 8 years
  • Maximum age of entry: 55 years
  • Minimum sum assured: Rs. 75,000

Premiums

The premiums for LIC Aadhar Shila and Aadhar Stambh Plans are based on the age and sum assured of the policyholder. The premiums can be paid annually, half-yearly, quarterly, or monthly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I apply for LIC Aadhar Shila and Aadhar Stambh Plans?
A: You can apply for these plans online through the LIC website or by visiting your nearest LIC branch.
Q: What documents are required to apply for these plans?
A: You will need to provide your Aadhaar card, PAN card, and address proof.
Q: Can I make changes to my policy after it has been issued?
A: Yes, you can make changes to your policy by contacting the LIC customer service.
